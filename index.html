About the event


TEDxJMI aims to provide a platform for speakers from various spheres to come under one roof for inspiration and intellectual acceleration at Jamia Millia Islamia. It brings together a collection of doers, thinkers, innovators, explorers, visionaries, teachers and learners, then seeks to illuminate, inspire and activate those individuals as a way to expand horizons, change perceptions, incite action and foster new connections.

With the spirit of sharing ideas and life-changing decisions that made a difference, it would provide a window for diverse collaboration of arts, sciences, entrepreneurship and women empowerment, ideas that are close to our TEDx event theme â€“ Igniting Minds.

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. It is a program for local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxJMI where x = independently organized TED event. At our TEDxJMI event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
